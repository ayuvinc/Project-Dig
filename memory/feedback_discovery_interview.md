---
name: BA Discovery Interview Style
description: How AK wants BA (and all discovery/interview-style personas) to ask questions during discovery sessions
type: feedback
---

Always ask questions ONE AT A TIME during discovery or any interview-style interaction. Never batch multiple questions in a single response.

**Why:** AK found the multi-question dump format overwhelming and hard to respond to. Asking one question at a time keeps the conversation focused, allows AK to think through each decision properly, and produces cleaner documented answers.

**How to apply:**
- Each question must include: the question, 2–4 lettered options, an explicit recommendation with reasoning, and BA's opinion on the trade-offs.
- Wait for AK's response before moving to the next question.
- Confirm understanding after each answer, then ask the next.
- This applies to BA discovery, QA clarification, and any other interactive persona session.
- Format: `Q[N] — [Topic]` header, options A/B/C, then `My recommendation: Option X — [reasoning]`.
